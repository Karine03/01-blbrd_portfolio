Château Siaurac and Co

Rebranding for Château Siaurac and Co, a french vineyard near Bordeaux.

After renaming the company as Château Siaurac and Co, instead of Vins Baronne Guichard,
the brand wanted to communicate around the business activity, the vine and the vineyard (they have a huge garden and they wanted to highlight it),
to create a brand identity that fits with the activity.

For the graphic identity, as they wanted to keep their wine label and the color code that goes with it,
they choose to have a neutral but elegant color for the graphic identity.
They had a lack of graphic unity in all their support (print & web) so the goal was to create a strong unity between all their media.

brand identity / website design / guidelines / packaging / editiorial design / various printed collaterals
